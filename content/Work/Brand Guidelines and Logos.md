# Summary
taking all brand guidelines into account, for uniform sizes use at least:

Minimum 29x29 pixels for the logo

Minimum margin of half the logo width z.B. 15 pixels on each side (=padding of 7.5 pixels)

Download: [﻿brand.linkedin.com/content/brand/global/en_us/index/downloads](https://brand.linkedin.com/content/brand/global/en_us/index/downloads) 

Guidelines:

[﻿brand.linkedin.com/content/brand/global/en_us/index/visual-identity/logo](https://brand.linkedin.com/content/brand/global/en_us/index/visual-identity/logo) 

Modern download/guidelines (not necessarily preferred, use judgement):

[﻿brand.linkedin.com/downloads](https://brand.linkedin.com/downloads) 



![5v58WHT3AVVCL0a6exalH.png](https://eraser.imgix.net/workspaces/35261U8rpe9tk30ShXap/aoy5ChwiXnhNli1HegJCElwlHTw1/R7iMUhPuInUlwU9kdEc1f.png?ixlib=js-3.7.0 "5v58WHT3AVVCL0a6exalH.png")

- The logo must be the original shape as supplied or the website. It may not be displayed as a circle, square or other shape. 
- The must be accompanied by a ™ symbol, so long as it is at a size where the ™ symbol would be legible. For email purposes that is not necessary. Only use the ® instead of ™ if the content is a post on the official linkedin website 
- The logo must be surrounded by free space on all sides equal 2𝑥, where 𝑥 is the width of the 'i' in the logo.
- Minimum 21x21 px, not including ™/® symbol
- If using the ™ or ® symbol, do not place directly to the left of instagram icon, as this may break instagrams 'free space' requirement. All other brand icons are ok.

- Minimum 29x29 pixels
- Use the "glyph", do not use the "app icon"


# Facebook:
[﻿about.meta.com/brand/resources/facebook/logo/](https://about.meta.com/brand/resources/facebook/logo/) 

![s3MhQiu8NFde2DDY2Hn7w.png](https://eraser.imgix.net/workspaces/35261U8rpe9tk30ShXap/aoy5ChwiXnhNli1HegJCElwlHTw1/g0G8ZSen3kocqpDmKppLz.png?ixlib=js-3.7.0 "s3MhQiu8NFde2DDY2Hn7w.png")

Free space must be at least one quarter of the icons size on all sides

- Minimum 16x16px
- Do not change the color
- Do not write the word Facebook next to the logo
- Do not just use the 'f', you must use the full logo
- Do not modify the logo, do not add an outline.


# YouTube:
[﻿www.youtube.com/howyoutubeworks/resources/brand-resources/#logos-icons-and-colors](https://www.youtube.com/howyoutubeworks/resources/brand-resources/#logos-icons-and-colors) 

![diagram-export-4.2.2025-14_34_34.png](https://eraser.imgix.net/workspaces/35261U8rpe9tk30ShXap/aoy5ChwiXnhNli1HegJCElwlHTw1/LFHzjUUnt3eMmaVyJPh1V.png?ixlib=js-3.7.0 "diagram-export-4.2.2025-14_34_34.png")

- The 'Logo' contains the word 'YouTube' while the Icon does not.
- The free space must be equal or greater than the triangle in the logo
- Must link to a youtube channel
- Supposedly requires special approval for any and all usage
- Youtube provides a circular version if you download the 'Icon' not 'Logo' (Full color icon/social/red/youtube_social_circle_red.png)
- Minimum height 20px (=width 28px)
- (#FF0000/#FFFFFF/#282828)
